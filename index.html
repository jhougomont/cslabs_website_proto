THE TEAM

John Tan, PhD

CEO & Founder

Dr. John Tan is Chief Executive Officer for SciX3, LLC (dba CSLabs) and is also a research affiliate at George Mason University. Dr. Tan previously served as the Chief Scientist for Mav6, where he was the company SME for physics-based modeling and simulation (M&S) and machine learning algorithms. He oversaw a team of physicists and programmers supporting the JIEDDO M&S branch for performing physics-based M&S, detection algorithm development, and associated HPC solutions. He performed IED detection sensor system analyses and assessments, including GPR and SAR, active directed energy RF devices, chemical and hyperspectral sensors, magnetic sensors, X-ray sensing systems, EO/IR sensors, additionally, he developed machine learning algorithms for imagery feature extraction and detection in EO/IR and hyperspectral images. Dr. Tan has over 25 years of professional experience, focusing on simulation of physical systems, sensor modeling, machine learning, and image processing.

Dr. Tan has a PhD in Computational Sciences, an MS in Applied Engineering Physics, and a BS in Physics from George Mason University.

Joshua Lunn

Lead Software Developer

Mr. Lunn is currently the lead software engineer at CSLabs. He is an accomplished software engineer, using his software design and development skills to build profitable commercial systems in various industries such as financial services, electric utilities, and online retail. Mr. Lunn has broad software development experience, including low-level firmware, mobile application development, web and database solutions, and AI/ML applications. He has developed firmware currently running on hundreds of thousands of devices with expected lifetimes over twenty years. Mr. Lunn also developed many highly successful commercial mobile apps, including the highest grossing app for hunters. Mr. Lunn holds a B.S. degree in Electrical Engineering from Mississippi State University.

Bryan Dorbert

Software Developer

Mr. Dorbert has been a software engineer at CSLabs since 2016 and is the current lead developer for the DeltaX software framework. He has been a lead developer for the OSPRay-based ray tracer used for propagation modeling, presented at IEEE GLOBECOM 2020. His past work has involved the development of CPU and GPU-based ray tracing technologies for simulation of X-ray imaging as well as supporting the development of TensorFlow-based deep learning software for the classification of scientific documents. Mr. Dorbert holds a B.S. degree in Computer Science from Mount St. Mary’s University.

Joshua Hill, PhD

Lead Analyst

Dr. Hill is an Associate Professor of Criminal Justice at the University of Southern Mississippi (USM) and Analyst with CSLabs. He has been Director of the Institute for Advanced Analytics and Security since its inception in 2018 and has served as PI/CO-PI on several large contracts and grants from the USG, including from NOAA and OUSD(P). His research has focused on the application of advanced statistical and machine learning methods to social science data, specifically looking at statistical analysis of language in political speech. He also has significant experience in UI/UX development for large data systems, having developed a number of decision support tools for NOAA. Dr. Hill holds a PhD from Sam Houston State University in Criminal Justice, which was received in 2012.
